Catholic Prayers: How to Pray the Rosary

The Holy Rosary includes many Catholic prayers

  • The Apostles' Creed
  • The Doxology
  • The Fatima Prayer
  • The Lord's Prayer
  • The Hail Mary
  • The Salve Regina

Prayers of the Rosary

The Apostles' Creed

I believe in God, the Father almighty, Creator of heaven and earth, and in Jesus Christ, his only Son, our Lord, who was conceived by the Holy Spirit, born of the Virgin Mary, suffered under Pontius Pilate, was crucified, died and was buried; he descended into hell; on the third day he rose again from the dead; he ascended into heaven, and is seated at the right hand of God the Father almighty; from there he will come to judge the living and the dead.

I believe in the Holy Spirit, the holy catholic Church, the communion of saints, the forgiveness of sins, the resurrection of the body, and life everlasting. Amen

The Doxology

Glory be to the Father and to the Son and to the Holy Spirit, as it was in the beginning is now, and ever shall be world without end. Amen.

The Fatima Prayer:

O my Jesus, forgive us our sins, save us from the fires of hell, and lead all souls to Heaven, especially those in most need of Thy mercy.

The Lord's Prayer

Our Father who art in heaven, hallowed be thy name. Thy kingdom come, Thy will be done on earth, as it is in heaven. Give us this day our daily bread, and forgive us our trespasses, as we forgive those who trespass against us, and lead us not into temptation, but deliver us from evil. Amen.

The Hail Mary

Hail, Mary, full of grace, the Lord is with thee. Blessed art thou among women and blessed is the fruit of thy womb, Jesus. Holy Mary, Mother of God, pray for us sinners, now and at the hour of our death. Amen.

The Salve Regina

Hail, Holy Queen, Mother of Mercy, our life, our sweetness and our hope! To thee do we cry, poor banished children of Eve. To thee do we send up our sighs, mourning and weeping in this valley of tears. Turn, then, O most gracious Advocate, thine eyes of mercy toward us, and after this, our exile, show unto us the blessed fruit of thy womb, Jesus. O clement, O loving, O sweet Virgin Mary. Lead: Pray for us, O holy Mother of God. Response: That we may be made worthy of the promises of Christ.

Steps for the Rosary

  1. Determine the day and whether it is lent, advent, or ordinary time
  2. Hold on the the cross, make the sign of the cross, and pray the Apostles' Creed
  3. On the first bead the lords prayer
  4. On the next three beads say the Hail Mary for increase in the virtues of faith, hope, and love
  5. In the gap before the next bead pray the Doxology, and announce the first mystery (see mysteries index)
  6. Pray the Lords Prayer on the next bead
  7. On the following ten beads prayer the Hail Mary while meditating on the mystery
  8. After the first decade pray the Doxology and the optional Fatima prayer
  9. Continue in this pattern until the end of the firth decade, here pray the Salve Regina
  10. Finally make the sign of the Cross
